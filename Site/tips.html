Tips

Before you throw out the food, take a look at some of these tips.

Bananas

Got too many bananas? Did you know the nutritional values in bananas help moisturize, add glow and have anti-aging benefits in them. Most importantly they help treat acne and the scars it can leave behind. Below is a common banana masks used for acne and scars.

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 a banana
  • 1/2 tablespoon baking soda
  • 1/2 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

Eggs

Are your eggs about to expire? Eggs have been used in hair masks for the longest time and are known for helping keep your hair moisturized and strong. They can also help hair growth too! Below is an egg mask used for dull and frizzy hair.

Ingredients:

  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon virgin coconut oil
  • 1 tablespoon full-fat yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
